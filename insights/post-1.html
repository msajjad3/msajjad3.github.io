How AI and Satellites Help Us Predict Flood Risks in Cities

Published: July 20, 2026 | By Dr. Muhammad Sajjad

Flooding is one of the most destructive natural disasters, and it's getting worse in cities. But here's the challenge: we often don't know where the flood will hit hardest, or who will be affected most. That's where our research comes in.

The Problem: Uneven Risk

In any city, some neighborhoods are much more vulnerable to floods. Why? It's a mix of geography, infrastructure, and socio-economics. Low-income communities often have fewer resources to recover.
