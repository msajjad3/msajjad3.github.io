Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS)

🇭🇰 Hong Kong ▼
Deadline December 1 (annually)
Type Fully Funded
Level PhD
Duration 4 Years

The Hong Kong PhD Fellowship Scheme (HKPFS) is a prestigious government-funded program that supports outstanding international students to pursue PhD studies at Hong Kong's top universities, including HKU, CUHK, HKUST, and PolyU.

Benefits: Annual stipend of HK$337,200 (approx. US$43,000) and conference/research travel allowance of HK$16,800 (approx. US$2,100) per year.

Eligibility: Bachelor's or Master's degree with excellent academic records, strong research potential, and proficiency in English. Open to all nationalities.

Top Universities: The University of Hong Kong (HKU), Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

Chinese Government Scholarships (CSC)

🇨🇳 Mainland China ▼
Deadline December - April (varies)
Type Fully Funded
Level Bachelor's, Master's, PhD
Duration 1-5 Years

The Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) is a prestigious scholarship program sponsored by the Ministry of Education of China. It supports international students to study at over 280 Chinese universities and research institutions.

Benefits: Full tuition coverage, comprehensive medical insurance, accommodation, and monthly living allowance (CNY 3,000 for bachelor's, CNY 3,500 for master's, CNY 4,000 for PhD).

Eligibility: Excellent academic record, good health, and proficiency in Chinese or English. Age limits apply (25 for bachelor's, 35 for master's, 40 for PhD).

Top Universities: Tsinghua University, Peking University, Fudan University, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Zhejiang University, and many more.
