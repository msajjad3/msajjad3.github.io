🌍 Scholarship Opportunities

Below is a list of prestigious and fully-funded scholarships for students and researchers. I will keep this page updated as new opportunities arise.

  • Fulbright Foreign Student Program

    Fully-funded scholarships for international students to study in the USA. Covers tuition, living stipend, and health insurance.

    📅 Deadline: Varies by country (usually Aug-Oct)

  • DAAD Scholarships (Germany)

    Offers for graduates and PhD students from developing countries to study in Germany. Covers monthly payments, travel, and insurance.

    📅 Deadline: Often between Aug - Dec

  • Chevening Scholarships (UK)

    Fully-funded UK government scholarships for future leaders. Covers tuition, living costs, and travel.

    📅 Deadline: Early November annually

  • Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degrees

    Highly prestigious, fully-funded master's programs offered by a consortium of European universities.

    📅 Deadline: Varies per program (often Oct-Jan)

  • QS Scholarships

    A comprehensive database of scholarships worldwide, including the QS Leadership Scholarship.

    📅 Deadline: Varies, check the website.
